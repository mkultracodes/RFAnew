Youth --    Ages 7 - 9


Sign up for the Full Beginner's Course! 8 Weeks / 1 or 2 classes per week based on age

Beginner Fencing program is designed to teach fundamental techniques and principles of fencing. Each class primarily focus on developing basic fencing skills such as:

  • Handling of the saber
  • Footwork and distance control
  • Fencing etiquette and good sportsmanship
  • Coordination and focus
  • Introduction to fencing rules

To significantly improve fencing skills, participants are recommended to take at least 2 lessons per week.

The beginner Fencing program is offered to all ages as early as 5 years old. RFA provides all necessary equipment to participate in the beginner program. Students are expected to attend in proper attire:

Teen --    Ages 10 - 13

Up to 2 classes per week with flexible schedule.

Intermediate level classes with introduction to competitive fencing and options to participate in regional and national tournaments. Each class is 1.5 hours long with primarily focus on advanced development of fencing skills such as:

  • Introduction to competitive fencing
  • Fencing tactics and strategies
  • Endurance and conditioning
  • Advanced footwork and fencing actions

To significantly improve fencing skills, participants are recommended to take at least 1 or 2 private lesson per week.

Typical participants age group is up to 12 years old with previous fencing experience or those that completed Beginner Fencing program. Participants are encouraged to have their own fencing equipment.
